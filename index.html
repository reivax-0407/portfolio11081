Game Development

These are the two games that made me think for the first time in my life "How would you have made a game like this?". These games were so interesting in their gameplay mechanics and the way they presented the story that it made me want to create games of my own one day that might have mechanics like these games.

The first game Hollow Knight is an indi game , basically a game made by a small studios that caters to only one niche of audiance. This game was made by Team Cherry(3 people)and was released in 2017. The mechanicas of this game is buttery smooth and at the same time challenging to the core. This game rivaled the big AAA titles of its time and won several awards for best indie game of the year.

The second game is Helldivers 2 which is a 3rd person shooter, this is the only game in the world where you can accidentally team kill your whole team and still laugh about it. The game is so fun to play with friends and the gunplay mechanics are so satisfying that i could play this game for hours on end. This game was developed by Arrowhead Game Studios and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. And the amount of updates and content they provide for free is mind blowing.
